About LandCheck

A free, open-source tool for evaluating raw land before purchase. Pulls public US data from FEMA, USFWS, USGS, USDA, and the US Census, plus parcel records from official state & county GIS portals — statewide where a state publishes one, otherwise the largest metro counties, plus Virginia via 30 localities. See the Coverage button for the full list.

No account and no analytics — your saved places and settings stay in your browser. Map clicks and searches are sent directly to the public data providers (OpenStreetMap, US Census, USGS, FEMA, USDA, and county GIS servers) to fetch results, and those services may log the request.

This is a research aid, not due diligence: boundaries are county GIS renderings, not surveys. Verify with a licensed surveyor and a title company before any purchase decision.

Built for landowners, by a landowner.
